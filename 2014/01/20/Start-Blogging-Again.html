Start blogging again

The last post (also the first one) was nearly 7 months ago. Gahh! I’m too lazy for writing blog. So why the heck should I start writing blog again?

I have met a grumpy bear recently, 2 months as I remember, things are always turning complicated when you meet a (grumpy) bear, whichever color it is. Days ago, she asked me something about viruses, security things, … I’m not a security expert but anyone who has interest in computer/information security should know that those questions can’t be easily answered in one (hundred) sentences; in these cases, all IT engineers wish sentence was an uncountable noun. The conversation with the bear raised some problems:

  • By searching wrong information resource, she thought (or may keep thinking) that computer bots (robots) are computer viruses.
  • She wants to be sure that her Windows system is clean and not infected by viruses, which is written by some creapy/creepy and oh-you-loser guys.
  • She want to be safe on the Internet.

Hmm.. I could just let it go if someone else asks me something like that. But, well, the conversation was just like a 10 tons snowball in OMG! shape going straight to my face. I give talks sometimes and they’re often under control; the bear has many super cute reaction (when she heard about viruses, bots, blah blah..) that I couldn’t manage to clearly tell her anything. So, I will spend a little time to share information and methods (from my poor knowledge) by writing some posts.
